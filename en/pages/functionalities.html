Technomarket System

Techomarket number 1 in technology



"Technomarket" has an administrative system for the purchase and sale of Computer products. This seeks to facilitate the management of all processes that are done in any other common enterprise, but at an extra expense of time and money. Therefore, our system performs processes which facilitate the management of record management depending on each module created within the system, which have intuitive interfaces that are easy to driving.


Customers

In this module you will be able to manipulate the data of the clients of Technomarket, either by adding, updating and/or deleting data from it, through information collected from them.



Products

The purpose of this module, in addition to facilitating, is to add all information of new products, to later manage said information incoming inventory, with the purpose of managing quickly and effective all kinds of order.



Inventory

The purpose of this module is to be able to keep a detailed control of each one of the products that our store sells as the amount of input and output products, dates of ordering and quantities with which the company works.



Employees

In this module it will be possible to maintain control of all new employees who be added to manage the system, taking into account that each employee has his own role as a user, he can have access to specific system services according to their assigned role.



Suppliers

The purpose of this module is to keep track of the suppliers to the which we use to obtain new products, keeping records of the places, addresses and types of products to receive.



Records

In this module, unlike the others, it is created solely for view all records of employees, suppliers and customers who they will be handled by other modules.



Orders

In this module, as its name indicates, a control of the orders that will be made through the suppliers that will be registered in its corresponding module. For the purpose of carrying in a way organized all the orders that are made day by day.
